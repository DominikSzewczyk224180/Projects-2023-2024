Introducing ATHENA

Overview

Athena is a cutting-edge Python package and cloud application designed for plant science researchers at the Netherlands Plant Eco-phenotyping Centre (NPEC). This powerful tool enables precise organ segmentation and landmark detection on high-resolution plant images, facilitating a deeper understanding of plant phenotypes. This tool allows research centres to train models on their own data and provides a high level of customisability, ensuring a precise analysis on various use cases within plant-phenotyping.

Why 'ATHENA'?

This application is a deployment and advanced tool built on NPEC's phenotyping installations designed for high-throughput phenotyping. NPEC's systems, called 'Helios' & 'Hades', capture high-quality data from plants above and below ground. Named after the Greek goddess of wisdom, Athena embodies (artificial) intelligence and strategic warfare, metaphors that align well with the application's goal to harness sophisticated analytical capabilities in plant science. Automating the labelling and analysis process, this application allows easy tracking of strategically modified environmental circumstances.
